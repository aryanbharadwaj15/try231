What We Do

  • Test and Validate: Automatically assess the quality of medicines and consumables using integrated lab devices and AI-based quality analysis tools.
  • Ensure Compliance: Verify that all incoming supplies meet national and international regulatory standards.
  • Monitor and Reject: Instantly flag and reject substandard or counterfeit products, ensuring that only trusted, high-quality supplies are accepted.
  • Support Healthcare Providers: Provide real-time reports, analytics, and alerts, helping hospitals and clinics make informed decisions about their medical supplies.

Our Commitment

At MedVerify, we believe in the power of technology to transform healthcare. Our platform is designed to eliminate risks associated with low-quality or counterfeit supplies and give healthcare providers the peace of mind that their products are safe and effective. We work closely with medical suppliers, regulatory bodies, and healthcare organizations to continuously improve our processes and deliver trusted, reliable results.

  • Quality First: We prioritize patient safety by ensuring that every product that passes through our system meets the highest standards.

  • Proactive Compliance: With automated rejection of low-quality supplies, we help healthcare providers maintain compliance without the need for manual oversight.
